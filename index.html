    About Me

    My name is Dan Svarczkopf. I received a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Secondary Mathematics Education from Mount Saint Mary College. I more recently graduated from the Educational Technology masters program at SUNY College at Oneonta. At my previous school I developed an educational plan to implement mobile education devices, Motorola Xooms, into the everyday classroom environment. This past year I developed a technology plan that will help my current school teach our transient population through individualized, self-paced learning. The plan included not only the teaching plan, but also the hardware infrastructure needed to support such an endeavor. My teaching philosophy is that every child is capable of learning how to learn. Hence, I do not seek to merely teach the material, but to train each child in the core principles of mathematics, such that they can apply them to problems and situations that they have not yet come across.
