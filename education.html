    SUNY Oneonta

    During my years at SUNY Onetona I earned a Bachelor’s of Science in Educational Technology. I immediately put this education to use by developing a district technology plan for Charlotte Valley CSD. In this plan, every student received a Motorola Xoom with a 4G high speed internet connection. This ensured every student in our district had a computer and an internet connection. This enabled the district to better prepare its students for 21st century jobs.
